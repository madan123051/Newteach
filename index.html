About Us

Trusted Insect Protection Experts Since 2008

NewTech Fly Screens is a premier manufacturer and supplier of high-quality insect protection systems in India. We specialize in designing and installing custom-fit fly screens that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal.

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility uses the finest materials — from durable aluminium and uPVC frames to high-grade fiberglass and stainless steel mesh — ensuring long-lasting protection for your home and office.

  • Custom-made to exact measurements
  • Premium quality materials & hardware
  • Professional installation service
  • 5-year warranty on all products
  • Pan-India delivery & service
